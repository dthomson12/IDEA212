Evaluation Critiera

Value Outcomes
5 The three personas you identified & summarized demonstrate an understanding of the differences between how a human would use the website.
10
  • Your persona includes characteristics from multiple persona types. Eg. Your fictional persona could also include goal oriented information, or role based details. You may present the information using "engaging" techniques that bring your persona to life.
  • Your persona follows best practices.
  • Your persona is realistic.
10 Your persona is presented in a way that clearly displays the different characteristics, is logically organized, and follows best practices. It is also visually pleasing to look at.
